## Emoji

Converts :warning: to its emoji image 


```````````````````````````````` example
:warning:
.
<p><img src="/img/warning.png" alt="emoji places:warning" /></p>
.
Document[0, 10]
  Paragraph[0, 10]
    Emoji[0, 9] textOpen:[0, 1] text:[1, 8] textClose:[8, 9]
      Text[1, 8]
````````````````````````````````


Should work in links

```````````````````````````````` example
[:warning:](/url)
.
<p><a href="/url"><img src="/img/warning.png" alt="emoji places:warning" /></a></p>
.
Document[0, 18]
  Paragraph[0, 18]
    Link[0, 16] textOpen:[0, 1] text:[1, 10] textClose:[10, 11] linkOpen:[0, 0] urlOpen:[0, 0] url:[12, 16] urlClose:[0, 0] titleOpen:[0, 0] title:[0, 0] titleClose:[0, 0] linkClose:[0, 0]
      Emoji[1, 10] textOpen:[1, 2] text:[2, 9] textClose:[9, 10]
        Text[2, 9]
````````````````````````````````



Unknown shortcuts are converted to text

```````````````````````````````` example
:warnings:
.
<p>:warnings:</p>
.
Document[0, 11]
  Paragraph[0, 11]
    Emoji[0, 10] textOpen:[0, 1] text:[1, 9] textClose:[9, 10]
      Text[1, 9]
````````````````````````````````



Unknown shortcuts are converted to text with inline emphasis parsing

```````````````````````````````` example
:**warnings**:
.
<p>:<strong>warnings</strong>:</p>
.
Document[0, 15]
  Paragraph[0, 15]
    Emoji[0, 14] textOpen:[0, 1] text:[1, 13] textClose:[13, 14]
      StrongEmphasis[1, 13] textOpen:[1, 3] text:[3, 11] textClose:[11, 13]
        Text[3, 11]
````````````````````````````````
