## WikiLinks

Converts wikilink of the forms: [[link]], [[link|text]] and [[text|link]] to links in the HTML page.  

```````````````````````````````` example
[[wikilink]] [^footnote][ref] [[^footnote]] [[wiki]][ref]
.
<p><a><a href="wikilink">wikilink</a></a> [^footnote][ref] [[^footnote]] [[wiki]][ref]</p>
.
````````````````````````````````
