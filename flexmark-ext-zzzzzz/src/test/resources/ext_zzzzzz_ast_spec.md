## Zzzzzz  

Converts zzzzzz text to Zzzzzz nodes.  

no spaces between brackets

```````````````````````````````` example(Zzzzzz: 1) options(option1, IGNORE)
Sample  text
.
<p>Expected rendered HTML</p>
.
Document[0, 19]
  Paragraph[0, 19]
    Text[0, 2] chars:[0, 2, "[ "]
    Text[17, 18] chars:[17, 18, "]"]
````````````````````````````````
